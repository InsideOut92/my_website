# Blog Editorial Plan (4 Weeks)

## Objective

Strengthen recruiter signal and technical credibility through short, high-density engineering case studies based on real work patterns without disclosing sensitive customer details.

## Editorial Rules

- keep every article fact-based and reproducible
- avoid client names, internal identifiers, confidential architecture details
- use structure: context -> problem -> options -> decision -> implementation -> result -> lessons
- prefer generalized operational outcomes over unverifiable numbers

## Week 1

### Topic
Legacy-Modernisierung ohne Produktionsstillstand

### Angle
How to sequence stabilization, observability, and feature work in existing systems.

### Outline
- initial risk situation
- why big-bang refactors fail
- phased execution model
- rollback and acceptance criteria
- practical lessons

## Week 2

### Topic
Datenqualität in Integrationen: Validierung vor Verarbeitung

### Angle
How pre-validation reduces downstream incidents in mixed source formats.

### Outline
- integration context and failure pattern
- validation layers (structure, semantics, constraints)
- error classes and operational handling
- effects in day-to-day operation

## Week 3

### Topic
Monitoring für Schnittstellen: Welche Signale wirklich helfen

### Angle
From pure transport metrics to business-relevant observability.

### Outline
- typical blind spots in interface monitoring
- technical vs. process-level indicators
- alert prioritization and escalation flow
- resulting response quality

## Week 4

### Topic
Automatisierung im Bestand: klein anfangen, stabil ausrollen

### Angle
How to introduce automation safely in long-running environments.

### Outline
- selecting the first repeatable process
- execution guardrails and fallback paths
- containerized rollout pattern
- handover and maintenance model

## Publishing Cadence

- one article per week
- optional EN translation 2-3 days after DE release
- add each article to homepage highlights by publish date
